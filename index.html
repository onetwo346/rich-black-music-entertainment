For Immediate Release

Acclaimed artist ACJ is set to release his latest track, "In My Soul," on May 1st. The song will be available exclusively on the Rich and Black website for 24 hours before its official release on all digital stores.

"In My Soul" is a powerful and introspective track that showcases ACJ’s unique musical style. The song is sure to resonate with fans of all genres.

"I’m excited to finally share 'In My Soul' with the world,” said ACJ. “This song is very personal to me, and I hope it connects with listeners on a deep level."

Fans can visit the Rich and Black website on May 1st to hear "In My Soul" before it’s released on all digital stores.
